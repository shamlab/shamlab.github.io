Sham Lab

We gain insight into the epidemiology and genetic mechanisms of complex traits and rare diseases by developing new statistical methods and applying them to large scale genetic data.

Complex Diseases and Traits

Most common diseases have a complex genetic architecture. We develop new statisical methods to uncover the hiden genetic causes of diabetes, schizophrenia and others.

Rare Diseases

We investigate rare genetic disorders to uncover previously unknown disease mechanisms in order to help patients and families alike.
